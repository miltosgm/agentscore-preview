Frequently Asked Questions

Can I find property listings on AgentScore?

No. AgentScore focuses on helping you find and vet trusted agents and developers. Once you've chosen your agent, they'll help you find properties (including ones not listed publicly).

Does Property Finder show agent reviews?

Property Finder does not focus on agent reputation or client reviews. It's primarily a property listing aggregator.

Which platform is better for investors?

Use both. AgentScore helps you find agents with investment expertise and developer track records. Property Finder helps you browse available investment properties. Start with AgentScore to choose your advisor.

Can I use Property Finder without an agent?

Yes, but you'll still need to work with someone eventually. Most properties require agent involvement for viewings, negotiations, and paperwork. Using AgentScore first ensures you choose the right agent from the start.

Are AgentScore reviews verified?

Yes. Reviews are verified and tied to actual client experiences. We don't allow fake or unverified reviews.
