Frequently Asked Questions

What are the best neighborhoods in Nicosia?

Engomi and Strovolos are popular for families. Aglantzia is close to universities. The Old Town (within the walls) is trendy for young professionals. Lakatamia offers good value.

Is Nicosia good for rental investment?

Yes, especially near universities and the business district. Rental yields can reach 5-7%, higher than coastal areas due to lower purchase prices and steady demand.
